üìù Collecting Feedback

In-App Feedback

Users provide feedback naturally through:

  • Comfort rating: Too cold / Just right / Too hot
  • Comments: Optional notes like "gloves were too heavy"
  • Clothing edits: What they actually wore vs. recommended

Weekly Check-In Survey

Send a quick survey each week covering:

  1. How many times did you use the app this week?
  2. Were the recommendations accurate? (1-5 scale)
  3. Any bugs or issues encountered?
  4. Any feature requests?
  5. Would you recommend this app to a friend? (NPS)
üí° Tip: Use a simple Google Form or Typeform for surveys. Keep them under 2 minutes to maximize response rate.
