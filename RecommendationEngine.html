üó≥Ô∏è Voting System

Once similar sessions are found, the app uses a voting system to pick clothing:

How Votes Work
  • Each similar session "votes" for the clothing items it used
  • Higher similarity = more votes: votes = ceil(score √ó 3)
  • Feedback sessions get 2√ó votes compared to CSV imports
  • The item with the most votes wins for each clothing category

üìà Confidence Calculation

The confidence percentage shown in the app reflects both the quality and quantity of matching sessions:

Confidence Formula
confidence = (sessionCount / 10) √ó 30%  +  avgSimilarity √ó 70%
  • 30% weight for session count (capped at 10 sessions)
  • 70% weight for average match quality of similar sessions
  • Maximum confidence: 100%
